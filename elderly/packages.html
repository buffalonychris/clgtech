FAQ

Are devices included?

Service fees only by default. We reuse what you have and spec remaining devices at fair market prices.

Remote vs on-site?

Both are available. Many installs are hybrid: remote configuration plus one focused on-site session.

Does this replace medical alert systems?

It complements them. We design escalation paths that can include medical alert devices, caregivers, and neighbors.

Can we pause alerts during quiet hours?

Yes. Quiet hours and presence-aware logic prevent alert fatigue while keeping safety intact.
