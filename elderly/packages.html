FAQ

Are devices included?

We reuse what you have and spec remaining devices at fair market prices. All devices in your quote will be owned by you! Not leased!

Remote vs on-site?

Many installs are hybrid: remote configuration prior to install, plus one focused on-site session.

Does this replace medical alert systems?

It complements them. We design escalation paths that can include medical alert devices, caregivers, and neighbors.

Can we pause alerts during quiet hours?

Yes. Quiet hours and presence-aware logic prevent alert fatigue while maintaining safety.
