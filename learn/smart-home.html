Great devices and apps you control directly. Powerful features; you still do the work via taps and routines.

Strengths

  • Wide device variety (lights, locks, sensors, AV)
  • App scenes, voice commands, basic automations
  • Affordable entry; DIY-friendly

Gaps

  • Apps become remote controls you must manage
  • Cloud outages or flaky routines
  • Hard to keep rules consistent across vendors
