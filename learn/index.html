What is Automation?

What is Automation? Choosing Systems Security Systems Smart Home Home Automation Voice Assistants Smart vs Automation Comparison

Automation is when your home responds to context—motion, time, light, presence, weather—so you don’t have to issue commands.

Core ingredients

  • Sensors (motion, doors, light, leak) feed events
  • Controllers run rules locally for reliability
  • Actors (lights, locks, HVAC, AV) carry out scenes

What it feels like

  • Hallway glows at 10% at night, never blinds
  • Doors lock when everyone leaves
  • Thermostat settles when you say goodnight
