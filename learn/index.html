Automation, Elevated — The CLGTech Standard.
This isn’t about gadgets. It’s about living in a home that anticipates your needs and adapts in real time—quiet, precise, and beautifully invisible. Lights, climate, security, and sound align so comfort isn’t performed, it’s presumed.

Core ingredients

  • Sensors (motion, doors, light, leak) feed events
  • Controllers run rules locally for reliability
  • Actors (lights, locks, HVAC, AV) carry out scenes

What it feels like

  • Hallway glows at 10% at night, never blinds
  • Doors lock when everyone leaves
  • Thermostat settles when you say goodnight
