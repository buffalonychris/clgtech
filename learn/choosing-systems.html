Choose Like a Pro — Built for Longevity, Not Hype.
The right system is the one that feels invisible, stays reliable, and grows with your life. We select platforms that respect your privacy, run locally, and won’t strand you when a cloud service changes its mind.

What we prioritize

  • Local control for speed and privacy
  • Solid device ecosystem (lighting, shades, sensors)
  • Professional-grade networking and power

Tradeoffs

  • DIY hubs are affordable but flaky under load
  • Cloud automations can stall during outages
  • Pro systems cost more but age gracefully

Trusted by discerning homeowners. CLGTech designs for reliability first—then luxury follows naturally.
