All Ideas

Every idea in one place. Use it as a menu—pick a few, then layer.

Starter

First Week: Goodbye

Trigger: Wall switch triple‑tap by exit
Effort: Low
What happens
  • Turn off non‑essential lights
  • Arm stay or away depending on presence
  • Thermostat eco mode
Typical devices: Smart dimmers, Presence, Thermostat, Alarm
Comfort

Morning Warmup

Trigger: Weekdays 6:30am or first motion
Effort: Low
What happens
  • Pre‑warm bathroom and kitchen lighting
  • Boost heat for 45 minutes
  • News flash briefing optional
Typical devices: Dimmers, Thermostat, Speaker/assistant
Safety

Garage Safety

Trigger: Garage opens after sunset
Effort: Low
What happens
  • Mudroom lights to 30% for 10 minutes
  • Door chime if left open >5 minutes
Typical devices: Garage opener sensor, Dimmers, Contact sensor

Effort: Low

  • Hall bath to 10% warm

Typical devices: Motion, Dimmers
