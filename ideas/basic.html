Starter

First Week: Goodbye

Trigger: Wall switch triple‑tap by exit Effort: Low

What happens

  • Turn off non‑essential lights
  • Arm stay or away depending on presence
  • Thermostat eco mode

Typical devices

  • Smart dimmers
  • Presence
  • Thermostat
  • Alarm
Comfort

Morning Warmup

Trigger: Weekdays 6:30am or first motion Effort: Low

What happens

  • Pre‑warm bathroom and kitchen lighting
  • Boost heat for 45 minutes
  • News flash briefing optional

Typical devices

  • Dimmers
  • Thermostat
  • Speaker/assistant
Safety

Garage Safety

Trigger: Garage opens after sunset Effort: Low

What happens

  • Mudroom lights to 30% for 10 minutes
  • Door chime if left open >5 minutes

Typical devices

  • Garage opener sensor
  • Dimmers
  • Contact sensor
Convenience

Kitchen Task Lights

Trigger: Counter motion + luminance Effort: Low

What happens

  • Under‑cabinet to 100% during prep
  • Fade to 25% idle

Typical devices

  • Motion
  • LUX sensor
  • Light strip
Safety

Bedtime Path

Trigger: Bedroom motion 11pm–6am Effort: Low

What happens

  • Hall bath to 10% warm
  • Auto‑off after 6 minutes

Typical devices

  • Motion
  • Dimmers
Utility

Mail/Package Alert

Trigger: Mailbox contact or porch motion Effort: Low

What happens

  • Discrete chime + phone note
  • Flash foyer lights twice (optional)

Typical devices

  • Contact sensor
  • Doorbell cam
  • Chime/light
