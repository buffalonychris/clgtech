Simple automations that deliver daily convenience without complexity.

Daily Routines

  • Goodnight: lock doors, arm sensors, dim lights
  • Morning: gentle lights on, coffee outlet power
  • TV Time: bias light + lamp dimming

Convenience

  • Pantry/closet auto lighting via door sensor
  • Mailbox/parcel sensor notifications
  • Weather-aware porch light (sunset/sunrise)

Safety Basics

  • Water leak alerts (kitchen, laundry, bath)
  • Smoke/CO alerts to phones and speakers
  • Power outage notification
