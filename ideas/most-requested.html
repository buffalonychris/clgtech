The highest-impact automations most homeowners ask for first.

Smart Entry

  • Video doorbell with person/package alerts
  • Smart lock with auto-lock and user PIN codes
  • Door open/left-open reminders

Lighting Scenes

  • Welcome Home, Goodnight, and Away presets
  • Sunset/sunrise scheduling with offsets
  • Occupancy-based hallway/night lights

Comfort & Safety

  • Thermostat schedules with Eco/Away modes
  • Leak sensors with automatic shutoff valve
  • Garage door automation with geofence reminders

Security & Coverage

  • Camera plan: driveway, porch, side yard views
  • Notifications when gates/garage open at night
  • Audit log for locks and entry events
