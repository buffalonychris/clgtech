Our most-requested automations, distilled. Each idea pairs a clear trigger with predictable outcomes that feel natural.

Daily Rhythm

Goodnight Scene

Trigger: Any bedroom switch double-tap after 10:30pm Effort: Low

What happens

  • Dim hallways to 10% warm
  • Arm perimeter, lock doors
  • Set thermostats to sleep profile
  • Enable quiet notifications

Typical devices

  • Smart dimmers
  • Door locks
  • Thermostat
  • Alarm panel
Safety

Pathway Lighting

Trigger: Motion at night + darkness Effort: Low

What happens

  • Guide lights to bathroom/kitchen at 5–15%
  • Auto-off after calm period
  • No activations during 'guest mode'

Typical devices

  • Motion sensors
  • Dimmers
Presence

Arrival Comfort

Trigger: Primary phone arrives + door unlock Effort: Medium

What happens

  • Welcome lights to 40% neutral
  • Comfort temp ramp
  • Optional: music in living area

Typical devices

  • Presence (Wi‑Fi/GPS)
  • Door lock
  • Thermostat
  • Speakers
Entertainment

Movie Night

Trigger: Remote macro / voice code / app tile Effort: Medium

What happens

  • Close shades, fade lights, set temp
  • Switch AVR/TV inputs and volume
  • 'Intermission' bump for breaks

Typical devices

  • AVR/TV
  • Shades
  • Dimmers
  • Thermostat
Protection

Leak Watch + Water Main

Trigger: Leak sensor trips Effort: Medium

What happens

  • Notify household + audible chime
  • Optionally close main valve
  • Lighting pulse to locate area

Typical devices

  • Leak sensors
  • Water valve
  • Chime/light
Security

Vacation Mode

Trigger: Away > 24h Effort: Medium

What happens

  • Presence simulation lights
  • Lower standby loads
  • Adaptive exterior lighting

Typical devices

  • Smart plugs
  • Dimmers
  • Outdoor lights
